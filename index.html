How to play Pig (dice game)ğŸ·

  • Players take turns rolling the dice.
  • Each roll adds to your current score.
  • If you roll a 1, your current score is lost and your turn ends.
  • You may choose to Hold to add your current score to your total score and pass the turn.
  • The first player to reach 50 points wins.
